    Learn to speak English with confidence

    English is a global language that connects businesses, travel and even the internet. However, many people around the world face barriers to learning English, including cost, distance, time, self-doubt and access.

    Do you want to learn English to...

    • Looking for a new job?

    • Do you want to go to university?

    • Are you looking to achieve a goal?

    • Do you serve in your community?

    • Do you communicate with family and friends?



    We can help you

    Join the thousands of people who speak English with confidence through our unique approach to language learning. An approach that includes personal study, daily practice, and in-person or virtual discussion groups.

    Here's how it works:

    Personal study

    Accelerate your learning by preparing for the discussion group. Access online or print resources for and study a learning principle, memorize vocabulary, and practice using model sentences.

    Chat groups

    Increase your confidence and develop relationships as you teach and learn together. Improve your speaking and listening skills while practicing in small groups or in pairs.

    Daily practice

    Use EnglishConnect study resources to develop a habit of daily practice. Look for ways to replace your daily activities to listen, speak, read and write in English.

    Choose your level and get started today

    With three options, EnglishConnect helps you start at the right level and grow with each step. After completing a level, you can advance to the next level or repeat a level to further improve your knowledge.
